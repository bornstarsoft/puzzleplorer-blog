The newly launched game AiliA by IceToad Studio LLC is captivating puzzle enthusiasts with its unique mirror-based gameplay. Available on Steam for $11.99, AiliA invites players into a stunning 3D world where they navigate between reality and a mirrored dimension, solving over 100 intricate puzzles that challenge their perception and creativity.

What sets AiliA apart is its innovative use of mirrors, which are not just visual elements but interactive spaces that players can manipulate. By adjusting mirrors, players can alter their environment, revealing hidden paths and solutions. This gameplay mechanic is not only engaging but also enriches the storytelling as players uncover the memories of sisters Aria and Lia throughout their journey.

With a free demo available, now is the perfect time for puzzle lovers to dive into this enchanting experience. Will you be able to master the art of reflection and unravel the mysteries within AiliA?

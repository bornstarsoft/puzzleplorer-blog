If you're a fan of indie games and haven't yet heard of "12, Memory Lane," you're in for a treat. Released on December 15, 2025, this free title has garnered "Very Positive" reviews on Steam, proving that not all great games come with a price tag. Set in the eerie estate of the late Henry Villemaine, players embark on a journey to uncover the mystery behind the disappearance of the Villemaine family, piecing together clues from journals and documents scattered throughout the game.

While some may categorize "12, Memory Lane" as a walking simulator, it cleverly intertwines puzzle elements that keep players engaged. With a runtime of approximately an hour and 45 minutes, it offers a compact yet emotionally resonant experience. The game has received minor critiques regarding its control scheme and length, but the overwhelming positive feedback highlights its storytelling prowess and artistic design.

Developed by students at Rubika Supinfogame under the name Forget-Me-Not Studio, this game is a testament to the talent emerging from educational institutions. As we look forward to their future projects, one can only hope they continue to deliver such impactful experiences. Have you added "12, Memory Lane" to your library yet? If not, now's the perfect time to dive into this captivating narrative.

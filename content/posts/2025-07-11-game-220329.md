In an exciting development for the gaming industry, My.Games has announced a strategic 50/50 joint venture with Crazybit, aiming to create a new wave of PC and console games. This partnership combines the expertise of both companies, with Crazybit's rich history in browser and mobile gaming and My.Games' commitment to high-quality game development. With one title already in early development, this collaboration is set to redefine the gaming landscape.

Crazybit, a Cyprus-based studio, is led by Boris Gertsovsky, who brings over 20 years of experience in the gaming industry. His vision for Crazybit focuses on leveraging AI and Emotional Design to create immersive gaming experiences. As both companies align their creative visions, they are poised to deliver groundbreaking titles that push the boundaries of what games can achieve.

As the gaming world eagerly anticipates the first title from this joint venture, it raises an intriguing question: how will this collaboration influence the future of game development? With their combined strengths, My.Games and Crazybit are certainly a partnership to watch in the coming years.

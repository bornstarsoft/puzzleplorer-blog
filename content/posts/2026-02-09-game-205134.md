The upcoming release of "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM" on February 10, 2026, promises to captivate puzzle enthusiasts with its innovative gameplay mechanics. Developed by solo creator stone, this point-and-click puzzle game invites players into a dreamlike world where they can rearrange objects like scraps of paper and leaves to uncover solutions. Unlike traditional puzzle games, the freedom to drag and drop items without fixed positions encourages creativity and exploration, making each puzzle a unique experience.

This third installment in the series not only builds on the success of its predecessor, which garnered a "Very Positive" rating on Steam, but also expands the puzzle count to over 40. Importantly, players new to the series can dive right in without needing prior knowledge of the earlier games. With its relaxing atmosphere and non-verbal gameplay, "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM" aims to provide a soothing yet intellectually stimulating experience.

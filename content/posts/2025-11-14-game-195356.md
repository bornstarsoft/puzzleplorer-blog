Pokemon Legends: Z-A has made headlines as the best-selling physical game in the US for 2025, showcasing the franchise's enduring popularity and market strength. According to Mat Piscatella from Circana, this latest installment not only leads in sales but also signifies Pokemon's dominance across various media categories, including toys and trading cards.

The game's impressive launch, achieving the highest physical sales since The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, highlights its appeal to both new and long-time fans. With 5.8 million copies sold globally in just its first week, Pokemon Legends: Z-A is poised to be a major player in the upcoming holiday season, further solidifying its place in gaming history.

As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how Pokemon Legends: Z-A continues to influence the gaming landscape and whether it can maintain its momentum in both physical and digital sales. Will this game set new records as the holiday season approaches?

In a strategic move to enhance its global gaming presence, JetSynthesys has acquired the popular merge puzzle game EverMerge from Big Fish Games. This acquisition is significant, as EverMerge has generated over $350 million in lifetime revenue and is ranked among the top 100 highest-grossing mobile games of all time. JetSynthesys aims to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to expand EverMerge into new high-growth markets, particularly in India.

The acquisition reflects JetSynthesys' commitment to building a diverse gaming ecosystem across various genres and regions. EverMerge's strong performance, particularly in the US and Western Europe, showcases its potential for sustained growth and engagement. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, it will be interesting to see how JetSynthesys integrates EverMerge into its broader strategy and what new features or expansions may be on the horizon.

In an exciting move for gamers, Sony has launched The Playerbase, a community program that gives players a unique opportunity to be scanned and featured in Gran Turismo 7. This initiative not only allows players to become part of the game but also fosters a deeper connection between the gaming community and PlayStation titles.

The Playerbase program invites players from various regions, including North America, Europe, and Asia, to apply for a chance to be featured in select first-party PlayStation games. Participants will need to sign in with their PlayStation account and share their gaming experiences. From there, finalists will undergo video interviews, with one lucky winner being chosen to appear as an in-game character portrait in Gran Turismo 7.

This initiative represents a significant step in how gaming companies engage with their communities, allowing fans to leave a lasting mark on their favorite games. As the program evolves, it will be interesting to see how other PlayStation Studios integrate fan features into their titles. Will you be applying to join The Playerbase?

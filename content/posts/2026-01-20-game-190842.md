If youre someone who finds joy in organizing and decorating, then the upcoming game Hozy might just be your new obsession. This delightful sandbox puzzle game combines elements of cleaning and decorating, reminiscent of favorites like Unpacking and PowerWash Simulator. The game allows players to tidy up and personalize various rooms, providing a satisfying escape into a world where order reigns supreme.

In Hozy, youll clean up rooms by swirling away garbage and sprucing up spaces with paint and wallpaper, followed by the fun of arranging furniture exactly how you like it. Unlike other games in the genre, Hozy gives you complete freedom in placing items, allowing for a unique and personal touch in every level. The ambient sounds create a relaxing atmosphere, making it easy to lose yourself in the process of creating your perfect space.

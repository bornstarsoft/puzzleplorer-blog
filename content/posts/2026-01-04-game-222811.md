The release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console Game marks a significant moment for fans of NASCAR gaming, especially with its impressive array of base paint schemes. This new installment not only features a diverse selection of drivers but also introduces the ARCA Menards Series to console gaming, enhancing the overall experience for players.

While the game has made strides in inclusivity, it does come with notable exclusions, such as the absence of Monster Energy cars and certain alcoholic beverage sponsors. However, the inclusion of iconic brands like Red Bull, making its return to NASCAR gaming after over a decade, brings a sense of nostalgia and excitement for long-time fans. 

As we look forward to future updates and expansions, one can't help but wonder what other beloved paint schemes and sponsors might make their way into the game.

Battlefield 6 has made headlines by clinching the prestigious title of Game of the Year at the UKIE Video Game Awards 2026, alongside accolades for Best UK PC Game. This first-person shooter, developed by Battlefield Studios with contributions from Criterion Games, has set a remarkable record by selling over seven million copies in just three days, marking it as the fastest-selling title in the Battlefield franchise.

The UKIE Awards not only celebrated Battlefield 6 but also recognized other standout titles like Atomfall and PowerWash Simulator, showcasing the diversity and creativity in the gaming industry. With Playstack winning Best Publisher for the second consecutive year and nDreams being named Best Developer, the event highlighted the ongoing evolution and innovation within the gaming landscape.

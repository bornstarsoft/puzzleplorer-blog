Exciting news for gamers! The beloved Far Cry 3 Classic Edition is set to receive a significant update that will enhance gameplay on the PS5 and PS5 Pro by introducing 60 FPS support. This update, confirmed by Ubisoft and shared via the official PlayStation YouTube channel, promises to deliver a smoother and more immersive experience for players revisiting this iconic title.

Originally released in 2012, Far Cry 3 has maintained a loyal fan base, and with over 10 million copies sold by late 2014, its clear that the game has left a lasting impact. While Xbox Series X and S players have already enjoyed the 60 FPS upgrade, PS4 owners have voiced their disappointment at being left out. Fortunately, this update is a step in the right direction for PlayStation users, allowing them to experience the game as it was meant to be played.

For those looking to dive back into the world of Far Cry, the game is available as part of the Far Cry Anthology Bundle on the PlayStation Store, currently at a reduced price. Will this update reignite the passion for Far Cry 3 among old fans and attract new players? Only time will tell!

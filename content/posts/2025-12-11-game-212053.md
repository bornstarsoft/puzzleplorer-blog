This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers are in for a treat with the addition of highly anticipated titles like *Assassins Creed Mirage* and *Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty* to the Game Catalog. These games, available for both Extra and Premium tier members, promise to deliver thrilling gameplay experiences that fans have been eagerly awaiting.

In addition to these blockbuster titles, the catalog also welcomes *Skate Story*, alongside family-friendly options such as *Paw Patrol* games and *Lego Horizon Adventures*. Notably, *Soulcalibur III* joins the Classic Catalog, exclusively for Premium members, allowing nostalgic gamers to relive the classic fighting action from the PS2 era.

With such a diverse lineup, this months offerings cater to a wide range of gaming preferences, ensuring that theres something for everyone. As we look ahead, it will be exciting to see how these titles perform and what future additions PlayStation Plus will bring to its ever-evolving library.

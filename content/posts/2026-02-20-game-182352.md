Kodansha Game Lab and Octorain have unveiled TrailRail, an innovative 2D action puzzle game that promises to redefine how players interact with their environment. With a unique mechanic that allows players to materialize their movement trails, TrailRail invites gamers to explore a world shaped by their actions, making each puzzle-solving experience deeply personal and engaging.

In TrailRail, players control Rail, a girl on a quest to uncover her lost memories. The game features 38 intricately designed puzzles that challenge players to think creatively and intuitively. With simple controlsMove, Jump, and Materialize Trajectoryplayers can manipulate their surroundings in fascinating ways, such as turning their flight path into a bridge or creating footholds to navigate the games challenges. This blend of physics-based gameplay and emotional storytelling sets TrailRail apart in the crowded puzzle game genre.

As players embark on this journey with Rail and her robot companion, they will not only solve puzzles but also uncover the mysteries of her past. Will you be able to navigate through the challenges and discover the truth behind Rails origins? The adventure awaits!

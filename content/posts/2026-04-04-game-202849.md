Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, Good Luck, is set to launch on April 8, 2026, for PC, with console versions for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S to follow. Developed by the innovative Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer promises a unique blend of chaotic gameplay and lighthearted humor, making it a must-try for fans of the genre.

In Good Luck, players will navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unpredictable hazards, all while racing to reach a meeting. The games mechanics are designed to challenge players, with no checkpoints and a focus on co-op gameplay for up to five players. Expect the unexpected as you dodge everything from banana peels to trash cans, all while battling chaotic physics that can turn any moment into a hilarious disaster.

As we look forward to its release, Good Luck stands out not just for its engaging gameplay but also for its potential to redefine the urban survival genre. Will you be brave enough to face the urban jungle?

The highly anticipated PlayStation Spectral Super Resolution (PSSR) upgrade is finally rolling out to PS5 users, promising to enhance gaming experiences across a variety of titles. This update, as announced on the PlayStation Blog, brings improved image stability, clarity in fine details, and more consistent performance for supported games, making it a significant enhancement for gamers looking to elevate their gameplay.

Initially introduced with Resident Evil Requiem, the PSSR upgrade will now support a range of popular titles including Silent Hill 2 Remake, Alan Wake 2, and Final Fantasy VII Rebirth, among others. While this upgrade is set to improve visual fidelity, Sony has cautioned that results may vary by title, and users will have the option to disable the feature if they encounter any issues.

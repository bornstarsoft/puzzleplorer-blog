In a surprising turn of events, YS Net, the studio founded by legendary game designer Yu Suzuki, is considering legal action regarding a purported Shenmue 4 trailer that has been circulating on social media. The studio has firmly denied any connection to the video, which allegedly showcases footage of the much-anticipated next installment in the Shenmue series. YS Net's statement highlights the importance of protecting intellectual property in the gaming industry, especially in an era where AI-generated content can easily mislead fans.

The studio clarified that the video in question uses their logo without permission, creating a false impression of authenticity. They emphasized that no official trailers or promotional materials for Shenmue 4 have been released, and they are taking this matter seriously. YS Net is currently working with relevant parties to address the situation and is preparing for potential legal measures to protect their brand and the expectations of their loyal fanbase.

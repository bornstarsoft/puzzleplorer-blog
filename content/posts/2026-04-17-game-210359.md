In a world where technology and emotion intertwine, "Save My Scrap" emerges as a compelling visual novel and puzzle game that invites players to repair a broken android while unraveling a poignant story. Set to launch on Steam on April 22, 2026, followed by console releases on Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 5, this game promises to deliver an immersive experience filled with rich narratives and stunning cyberpunk visuals.

Developed by Unio Anou and published by AMATA Games, "Save My Scrap" builds upon the success of its predecessor, "Fix My Junk." This upgraded version introduces new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode, enhancing the emotional depth of the gameplay. Players will not only engage in mechanical repairs but also forge connections that revive long-buried memories, ultimately confronting heart-wrenching truths.

As we anticipate the release, the question remains: how will the blend of storytelling and gameplay in "Save My Scrap" redefine our understanding of relationships in a technologically advanced world? Let's keep an eye on this title as it approaches its launch date!

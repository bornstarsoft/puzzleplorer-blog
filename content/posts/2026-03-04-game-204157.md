Accidental Studios is set to captivate gamers with their upcoming psychological horror puzzle game, Subliminal, launching on March 31st, 2026. Inspired by the eerie mythology of liminal spaces and The Backrooms, this game promises an unsettling experience that challenges players' perceptions of reality. With a playable demo already available on Steam, players can dive into a world where memory is unreliable and light manipulation is key to survival.

Built using Unreal Engine 5, Subliminal features hyper-realistic visuals and dynamic environments that shift as players explore. The games core mechanics revolve around perspective-based puzzles that distort spatial logic, forcing players to confront their subconscious fears. As players navigate through familiar yet twisted environments, they will uncover hidden pathways and reshape reality itself, all while grappling with the haunting atmosphere that lingers in the silence.

With its unique blend of psychological horror and intricate puzzle-solving, Subliminal is poised to leave a lasting impression on the gaming community. As we await its official release, one can't help but wonder how this innovative approach to storytelling and gameplay will redefine the genre. Will you be ready to face your fears in this distorted psychological landscape?

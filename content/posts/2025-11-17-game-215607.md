The world of gaming is about to get a thrilling new addition with the announcement of "They Will Come," a steampunk horror adventure puzzle game set for release on PC via Steam in 2026. Developed by Game Pop Studio, led by industry veteran Jeff Troutman, this game promises to immerse players in a visually stunning alternate history where they must navigate a dark narrative filled with puzzles and peril. 

In "They Will Come," players will take on the role of Little Benjamin, armed with a trusty slingshot and an electrobanger, as he faces off against towering automatons and the ominous Wretched Ones aboard a massive steampunk airship. The gameplay focuses on exploration and stealth, requiring players to collect items and solve intricate puzzles while avoiding detection. The bittersweet storyline and rich lore are sure to captivate fans of both horror and adventure genres alike.

As we await its release, the anticipation builds for what promises to be a unique blend of steampunk aesthetics and horror elements. Will "They Will Come" redefine the adventure puzzle genre? Only time will tell.
